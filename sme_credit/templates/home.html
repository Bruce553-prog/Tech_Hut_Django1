What is Credit Risk Management?

Credit risk management involves evaluating the likelihood that a borrower or counterparty will default on financial obligations. It helps financial institutions, businesses, and analysts make informed lending decisions by assessing factors such as credit history, repayment capacity, and exposure to financial risk.

Who Is It For?

This system is designed for banks, microfinance institutions, credit officers, and data analysts who need reliable tools to monitor borrower performance, predict default risk, and ensure compliance with regulatory standards.
